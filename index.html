Current Research Focus

Single-Cell Foundation Models for Drug Discovery

I'm pushing the frontier of single-cell foundation models to predict how immune cells respond to drugs and genetic perturbations. This work aims to dramatically accelerate target identification and reduce the time from discovery to clinic.

Key Research Areas:

  • AI-Driven Target Discovery: Using foundation models to identify novel therapeutic targets in inflammatory diseases
  • Immune Cell State Transitions: Mapping how immune cells change states in disease contexts (COVID-19, sepsis, lupus)
  • Multi-modal Integration: Combining single-cell transcriptomics, spatial biology, and clinical outcomes
  • Computational Perturbation Screening: In silico prediction of cellular responses to therapeutic interventions
  • Protein Structure-Function: Integrating AlphaFold predictions with functional genomics
