Xinru Qiu, PhD

Foundation Model Evaluation × Drug Discovery

I evaluate AI models so you know which outputs to trust.

Built systematic benchmarks comparing 4 single-cell foundation models (UCE, scGPT, Geneformer, TranscriptFormer) on 226K cells. UCE achieves 0.876 AUC for COVID severity prediction. Now building AI agents that automate the path from patient data to therapeutic targets.

Currently a Postdoctoral Researcher at UC Riverside (Godzik Lab), I apply foundation models to single-cell, multi-omic, and protein data to solve problems in immunology, infectious disease, and therapeutic discovery.

Core Competency: I know when AI outputs are right and when they're wrong. 10 years of biology training + hands-on foundation model experience = I can evaluate what others can only run.

I obtained my PhD in Genetics, Genomics, and Bioinformatics from UC Riverside in 2023, advised by Professor Adam Godzik. Download my resume.
