Hi! I am currently a Bioinformatics Postdoctoral Researcher at the University of California, Riverside.

I obtained my PhD in Bioinformatics from University of California, Riverside (UCR) in 2023, advised by Professor Adam Godzik. Here are my CV, Github, and Google Scholar pages.

Research interests

My research focuses on computational biology and bioinformatics, with an emphasis on the following areas:

  • Computational Biology and Machine Learning: Applying advanced machine learning techniques and single-cell transcriptomics to identify disease-specific signatures and biomarkers in conditions like COVID-19, sepsis, and autoimmune disorders.
  • Bioinformatics and Data Analysis: Analyzing large-scale genomic and transcriptomic datasets to uncover patterns in immune cell populations and their dynamics across various diseases.
  • AI in Structural Biology: Exploring the applications of AI technologies like AlphaFold2 for protein structure prediction and its implications in drug discovery and development.
  • Systems Biology Approaches: Integrating multi-omics data to understand complex biological systems and disease mechanisms, with a focus on immune responses and inflammation.
  • Translational Bioinformatics: Leveraging computational findings to identify potential biomarkers, therapeutic targets, and personalized treatment strategies, particularly in immunology and oncology.
